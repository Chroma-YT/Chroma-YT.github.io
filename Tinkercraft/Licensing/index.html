Licensing

What a mess!

Tinkercraft takes resources from many (open-source, publicly licensed) resource and data packs to expand its library. One side effect of this, however, is the many different licenses from with these packs spring. This page is dedicated to fulfilling the licensing requirements for all packs on Tinkercraft.

Tinkercraft Core License

This license describes how all packs made explicitly for Tinkercraft (By Tinkercraft devs) are to be licensed under.


You CAN:

- Use your custom Tinkercraft pack(s) for private use or distribution.
- Feature your packs in videos without crediting Tinkercraft.
- Publicly distribute your Tinkercraft pack, provided that it is distributed through a Tinkercraft download link.
- Publish a modified version of a Tinkercraft pack under any license, provided that you give Tinkercraft a copy of your modification to add to their library.


You CANNOT:

- Publicly distribute Tinkercraft packs without explicit authorization from Tinkercraft.
- Claim legal ownership of your Tinkercraft pack.
- Publicly distribute your Tinkercraft pack in its unmodified form, whether it is for profit or not (Profit includes ad money!).
- Use Tinkercraft packs in any form, directly or indirectly to train any AI or neural network model.


NOTES:

- Tinkercraft reserves the right to change or override this license on an individual or public basis at any time.
- Tinkercraft reserves the right to restrict or prevent usage by any one individual or group for any reason.

Assorted Goodies

This is a category for pretty much everything else. If I cannot find the license for something, or an idea for a pack is so simple it simply cannot be owned (Think Smoother Fences, it does nothing except rotate some textures), or there are so many different versions of a certain thing that it is ubiquitous (Think Dark UI, there are thousands of Dark Mode packs out there), then they go here! Please open a bug report if you find any errors or wish to clarify the licensing on your project on our GitHub.
