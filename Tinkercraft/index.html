Grand World

Before ------ Move the slider! ------ After

This pack expands and amplifies your world! You will find taller mountains, underground aquifers and rivers of both water and lava. By the coast, you can find dramatic cliffs, sometimes as tall as 50 blocks, and you can even find many smaller upgrades to individual biomes, such as desert dunes or carved mesas. This pack fixes "lerp issues," or the weird staircasing you sometimes will find on hills when the game fails to smooth out your terrain properly.

  • Built-in function to double world height for more detailed terrain!
  • Completely server-side!
  • No mods required!
